    Description
    Neo, a trendy tiffin from Milton is an insulated lunch box with a softer and light weight outer jacket. It contains 4 round leak - proof containers. Containers are microwaveable with steel inner. It's available in trendy jacket colour options and is ideal for Office, Workplace, Schools and Colleges. Containers inside are meant only for reheating purpose in microwaveable mode without lid and the reheating time should be maximum 2 minutes. Not advisable for Grill, Convection and Combination mode and food inside should not be wrapped in foil while reheating.
